    Comedy movies

    A comedy film is a category of film which emphasizes Humor. These films are designed to make the audience laugh in amusement . Films in this style traditionally have a happy ending (black comedy being an exception). Comedy is one of the oldest genres in the film and it's derived from classical comedy in theatre.
